About Us

Gushou was created by a dragonboat-centric team with the goal of connecting and growing the dragonboat community through a reliable and trusted source.

Gushou is an intuitive and integrated platform designed for efficient and effective dragonboat organization - increasing accessibility and improving the dragonboat experience for all.

  • connecting and growing the dragonboat community
  • improving the dragonboat experience
  • increasing accessibility to dragonboating

Why Gushou?

Gushou, means ‘drummer’ in Mandarin.

The beat of the drum beats produced by the drummer may be considered the pulse of the dragonboat.

The capabilities and opportunities provided by Gushou may be considered the pulse of dragonboating.
